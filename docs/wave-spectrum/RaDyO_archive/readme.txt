**********
**README**
**********

The attached '.mat' files contain full spectra and probability density functions computed from the short wave fields observed during the 2008 RaDyO campaign. Water surface elevation time series from the Riegl lidar are provided (with dimensions of meters).

For all files:

k - wavenumber in rad/m
B - omnidirectional saturation spectrum in rad
ustar - friction velocity u* in m/s
